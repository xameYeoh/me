Internship location

About me

I am a student in Bulgarian Technical university of Varna. I am currently in the 4th term of Software and Internet technologies program.

During my studies I happened to use Erasmus+ program to travel to the Netherlands to study ICT at Fontys Hogeschoolen in the city of Eindhoven. This experience left me with a ton of positive emotions.

While I was there, I was studying and working with multicultural team of students. A lot of out projects there were done for small local business owners, it was a win-win situation for both parties: Us, the students, got to know how to work with client in an Agile way, while the clients got their small website, application or game.

I really liked this approach, after that I was sure: the best way to learn something is to do it in Business environment, there you can get the right feedback and apply your knowledge right away. This is why I want to continue my Erasmus journey - I want to contribute and get experience, learn new skills and cultures, be part of a bigger team.
